Emmanuel Rodriguez

I am a second year PhD student working on direct and inverse modeling of laser-cut meta-materials as part of the European project ADAM2. Under the supervision of Stefanie Hahmann, Mélina Skouras and Georges-Pierre Bonneau.

Research activities

Physics-based modeling of meta-materials. (FEM and others reduced models) Inverse modeling : optimization of a model’s parameters in order to reproduce a given target shape. Realization of wood or plastics meta-materials to validate our work. (fablab : amiqual4home)
